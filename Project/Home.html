PUBLISHER DOWNLOADS CONTACT

  1. Original story: Access to the original story. Player can select a game mode of either Casual Story, Deep Story, or Another Story. Zen, Yoosung, and Jaehee's route are accessible through Casual Story; 707 and Jumin's route are accessible through Deep Story; V's and Ray`s route are accessible through the Another Story.

  2. Chatroom: This is where the main conversation happens and where all of the player's choice affect the storyline. Some days and routes have a visual novel mode, where the story is told in either first or third view perspective in visual novel style.

  3. Text messages: Takes the shape of a real text messaging system. It is an extra that doesn't really affect the storyline, but the player can gain hearts, heartbreaks, or nothing - depending on what choices they make for the reply. The player can answer the texts sent by any of the RFA members only to a limit set by the game.

  4. Phone calls: Players are able to receive an incoming call from one of the 5 characters after some chatrooms or visual novels. The phone will ring for 10 seconds. If the player doesn't pick up during the time limit, the phone call will remain as a missed call and the player is able to call them back after the next chatroom is available, most of the incoming calls. Players are also able to make an outgoing call to one of them in a specific time. They will either pick up or entering a voicemail when they're busy.

  5. Emails: To invite guests into party, the player needs to invite as many guests as possible. To have them attending the party, the player needs to answer their questions correctly. Answering 3 right questions will guarantee they come, while answering only two questions right will get you a 50-50 chance.

  6. Extra: After clearing an ending, player is able to access these chat rooms, unlocked CGs, and guests mails for the second time.
